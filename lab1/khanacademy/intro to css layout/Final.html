About the Theme

This page is inspired by the anime Your Lie in April, a story that intertwines classical music with profound themes of grief, friendship, and personal growth. The color palette reflects cherry blossoms (soft pinks), deep blues of melancholy, and warm accents symbolizing hope and passion.
